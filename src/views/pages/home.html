travel

Shoreditch is where we met, live and work so it was a no-brainer that we chose to get married here. We’re really looking to seeing our favourite people in the place we call home.

by train

If you’re coming from The North - the cheapest tickets usually come out three months before. It’s best to set up an alert for these on the Trainline. As we don’t get married ’til 2pm, you could catch an early train, or a Friday - Sunday return will probably cost £50.

Night do people - a Saturday - Sunday return will probably cost £45.

by tube

If you get the train into Kings X, an uber will probably cost £7-10, or you can get the Northern Line northbound two stops to Old Street (make sure the train is ‘via Bank’).

Liverpool Street station is a ten minute walk and Shoreditch High Street station is just round the corner.
