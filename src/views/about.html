My current role covers a wide range of fields: UX & UI design, interaction design, product design and also frontend development.

What that means is I’ll take a problem that our clients have and work with their customers to validate that the problem exists, then work on a solution; quick prototyping to test on end users. I’ll then work this into a high fidelity mock-up for approval before styling the HTML/CSS/SaSS. During development I’ll work closely with the team to ensure that the output is just right.
