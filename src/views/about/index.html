I’m a Product Designer at Geckoboard, I have over 8 years experience in the industry and working in small companies has given me experience in all aspects of the product process.

I’m adept at product research, user research, prototyping, UX and UI design, and love to user test my solutions with customers.

For a long time in my career I was the only woman. Realising this was the norm for a lot of us, I co-founded a community for womxn in Tech called Triangirls.

We meet monthly to discuss the topics many shy away from; f*ck ups, masturbation and pay negotiation. Yes. really. These are all topics we’ve run events on.

I’ve also writted for for NetMag, been interviewed by Vice and spoken at companies like Samsung.
